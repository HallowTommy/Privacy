1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how 2025 TimeRush Inc. (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information when you use the TimeRush mobile application (“App”).

By downloading or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
2. App Description
TimeRush is a personal productivity and reminder application that helps users organize and track recurring actions and tasks.

The App allows users to:

- create and manage personal actions
- add action names and descriptions
- assign actions to categories
- set reminders for actions
- add optional photos to actions
- mark actions as completed
- view action history and progress
- use predefined action templates

The App is designed for personal use and works directly on the user’s device without requiring account creation.
3. How the App Works
The App allows users to manually input information such as:

- action names
- reminder dates and times
- categories
- optional photos
- completion history
- progress records
- user preferences

This information is used only to provide the core functionality of the App.

All data processing is performed locally on the user’s device.

The App does not require an internet connection for its core functionality and does not connect to external servers for data storage or processing.
4. Data Collection
TimeRush does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to the developer.

All user-entered data:

- remains on the user’s device
- is not accessible to the developer
- is not shared with any third parties

The App:

- does not require registration
- does not request personal information such as name, email address, or phone number
- does not create user accounts
- does not collect payment information
- does not collect location data
5. Local Data Storage
The App stores information locally on the user’s device, including:

- created actions
- reminder settings
- categories
- optional photos added by the user
- completion history
- progress records
- app preferences

This data:

- remains stored only on the device
- is protected by standard device security mechanisms
- is not transmitted externally
- is not accessible to the developer

Users are responsible for managing and deleting their local data through the App or device settings.
6. Photos
TimeRush may allow users to add photos to actions.

Photos are used only for personal organization inside the App.

The App does not upload photos to external servers.

The App does not analyze, share, sell, or transmit user photos.

Any photos added by the user remain stored locally on the user’s device.
7. Notifications
TimeRush may use local notifications to remind users about actions they create.

Notifications are generated locally on the user’s device based on the reminder settings selected by the user.

The App does not send notification data to external servers.

The App does not use notifications for advertising or marketing purposes.
8. Tracking and Analytics
TimeRush:

- does not use analytics tools
- does not track user activity across apps or websites
- does not include third-party SDKs that collect data
- does not use advertising frameworks
- does not collect device identifiers for tracking purposes
9. What the App Does Not Do
The App:

- does not collect personal data
- does not transmit user data over the internet
- does not include ads
- does not sell or share any user information
- does not include social features
- does not allow users to communicate with other users
- does not provide public profiles
- does not use location tracking
- does not upload photos or action data to external servers
10. Data Security
We take reasonable measures to ensure that the App operates securely.

Since user data is stored locally on the device, its protection depends on the standard security mechanisms of the user’s device, such as device passcode, biometric protection, and operating system security.

However, no system can guarantee complete security of data stored on any device.
11. Children’s Privacy
TimeRush is not intended for users under 13 years old.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Since the App does not collect personal data, we do not knowingly store or process information from children.
